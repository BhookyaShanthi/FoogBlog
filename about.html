Feel the extraordinary love with our dishes. We have millions of fallowers.

Everything began in 2020 when the Shanthi Rathod opened her first espresso and doughnut and sandwich shop. It was very fruitful and this drove her to begin another shop called FOOD BLOG. She opened the principal store in 2021 in tripura in the INDIA.

Today, ‘FOOD BLOG’ can be found in 3 states in INDIA in excess of 10,000 areas. It has turned into the 'Indias' Coffee and Baked Goods chain, serving in excess of 9 million doughnuts and 1.5 some espresso a year all-inclusive.

At ‘FOOD BLOG’, you can appreciate a wide determination of doughnuts, espresso, drinks, and sandwiches all kind of food varietes that will have you shipping and slurping and more.

Let's Know More

Cooking is about passion

I’ve always had a passion for cooking. When I was a little girl I loved to cook and enjoyed learning about all sort of cuisines. Cooking is pure joy and fun for me. I cook as a way to ground myself, to think clearly and to relax for a change. I enjoy researching ingredients I’ve never heard of before, what pairs best with this spice or that vegetable. When someone strikes up a conversation with me about cooking, I could go on for hours, so be prepared! Through cuts, burns, failed recipes, forgotten ingredients, I still find joy in the art of cooking. Losing this passion would surely be like losing myself.
